Belgian keyboard - international layout ("BX")
==============================================

Linedraw, block and shade characters, the soft hyphen  and  the  non-breaking
space characters are not available.

Below is the drawing of the enhanced alphanumeric block.

  French, Fula, Soninke, Wolof, Kirundi, Swahili,  Kinyarwanda,  Somali,
  French Patois and Portuguese.
  Widely spoken: Spanish, Italian, Dinka, Nuer, Afar, Oromo and Luganda.

  Codepage: 30024
